Overview

DrugGEN is an AI-powered platform designed to accelerate drug discovery by generating novel drug candidates using advanced generative models.

View on GitHub

Features

  • Generative AI for drug candidate generation.
  • Integration with molecular databases.
  • Interactive visualization of molecular structures.
  • Scalable and user-friendly interface.
  • AI-driven analysis of drug interactions and side effects.
  • Predictive modeling for drug efficacy and toxicity.

      Healthcare Impact

      The integration of AI in drug discovery has revolutionized healthcare by reducing the time and cost of developing new medicines. Key impacts include:

      • Faster identification of potential drug candidates.
      • Personalized medicine based on genetic profiling.
      • Early detection and prevention of diseases using AI insights.
      • Improved drug safety through AI-driven risk analysis.
